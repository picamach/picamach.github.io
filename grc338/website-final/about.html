About Us

Nestled in the heart of Templeton, California, our café was born from a simple but deeply rooted passion: creating thoughtful, handcrafted drinks—especially matcha—and sharing them with our community. What began as a personal love for the art of curating flavors has grown into a place where people can slow down, enjoy something special, and feel at home.

From the start, our mission has been to introduce the Central Coast to the beauty of matcha. We take pride in crafting drinks that highlight its flavor, quality, and versatility, giving both longtime fans and first-time sippers something to love. Every cup is made with care, intention, and a genuine excitement to share something we’re proud of.

Being located in a small town means everything to us. Templeton’s charm, warmth, and sense of community are at the heart of who we are. We love keeping things local—supporting nearby makers, connecting with our neighbors, and becoming a spot where familiar faces stop in for their daily drink or discover a new favorite.

More than anything, we’re grateful to be the spot where people choose to gather, unwind, and enjoy a drink crafted just for them. Seeing someone try matcha for the first time—or return for the hundredth—reminds us why we started. This café is more than a business; it’s a passion project built for the community we love.
